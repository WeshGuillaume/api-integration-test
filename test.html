MyPersonalToken


MyPersonalToken will be able to use your account for a day (or until you disable it).

MyPersonalToken is not affiliated with Trello, and by permitting access to your content you assume all related risks and liabilities.

MyPersonalToken will be able to:
  • Read your name and username
  • Read all of your boards and teams
  • Read your teams' Power-Ups
  • Read your Enterprises

MyPersonalToken will not be able to:
  • Create and update cards, lists, boards and teams
  • Make comments as you
  • Read your email address
  • See your Trello password
  • Update your teams' Power-Ups
  • Update and manage your Enterprises
